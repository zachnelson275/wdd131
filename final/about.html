About Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is an online first-person shooter game with looter shooter and MMO elements. Released as a sequel to Destiny in 2017, Destiny 2 follows you, the Gaurdian, as you defend the Last Safe City on Earth. Fending off various alien races, you discover the truth about who is behind all the attacks, and how you can defeat it.

About this Site

This website is designed to help new Destiny 2 players get up to speed on the terminology found in end game activities, specifically the 6-player raids. Many of the words used are common English words, but in Destiny 2 they take on new meaning. This site aims to explain the meaning behind these words in a raid setting, and also give some common use cases for how to use these words. This is not meant to be a comprehensive list, just some of the basics.
