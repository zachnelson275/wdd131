Destiny 2 Terminology Site Plan

Zach Nelson

WDD 131.01

Overview

Purpose

This site will introduce Destiny 2 raiding terminology in an interactive and easy to digest way

Audience

Target audience is newer Destiny 2 players who want to get into raiding but are overwhelmed by the vocab

Dynamic elements

Each term will be placed on an interactive card that will expand to include the typical use definition and use cases. The card will start out as just the word, and when clicked will expand to include all information necessary to understand the term.
